How do I place an order?

Once you have found your preferred product, just follow the steps below:
• Select a preferred colour by clicking on the dropdown menu that shows your preferred colour and then clicks on ‘Add to cart, to add this product into your cart.
• You see a message in green saying your item has been added to your shopping cart.
• Click on the ‘proceed to checkout’ tab to complete your order process.
• Click on the ‘proceed to checkout’ tab to complete your order process.
At checkout, you will need to choose either to checkout as a guest or register
• If you click register and checkout or checkout as a guest, you will need to fill in your personal details and shipping address if you are a new customer.
• Click on your preferred payment option before clicking the ‘Place Order button

SHIPPING AND DELIVERY;
Do you deliver outside Lagos?
Logo.ng delivers anywhere within Nigeria, this is possible by a good business partnership with our courier service companies.

What is your estimated delivery time?
Lagos state within 24 hours after confirming the order, other states 1-3 business days.

OUR PROMISE! 72 Hours Nationwide Delivery Guaranteed (Applies To Paid Orders Only).

How much do you charge for delivery?

There is free delivery in Lagos state for orders with the amount above N100,000 (One hundred thousand naira only) and N1,000 (One thousand naira only) for orders with the amount below N100,000.
Southern states, a flat rate of N3,500 (Three thousand five hundred naira only) and Northern states, a flat rate of N4,500 (Four thousand five hundred naira only)
